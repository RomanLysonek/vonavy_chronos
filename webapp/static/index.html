What would justify another attempt

A rerun is warranted only if the evidence base or learning protocol materially changes.

More representative domain dataMore products, longer histories, additional event cycles, or comparable retail panels would test whether transfer improves with broader support.
Development-only adaptationA pre-specified fine-tuning or calibration protocol must be selected without looking at a new final audit.
Richer business contextInventory quantities, traffic, product metadata and planned events could reduce the covariate mismatch rather than asking a target model to infer local regimes.
A new untouched auditThe consumed audit cannot become fresh evidence again. A future claim needs newly reserved origins and a pre-registered success threshold.
